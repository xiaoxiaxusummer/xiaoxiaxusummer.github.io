## About Me

I am a **Postdoctoral Researcher** at **Queen Mary University of London**, working on:

- 6G antenna systems and multiple access
- AI-native wireless communications
- Large AI models over wireless
- Mobile Edge Generation (MEG)

I received my **B.Eng.** from the **School of Remote Sensing Information Engineering, Wuhan University**, in 2017, and my **Ph.D.** from the **School of Electronic Information, Wuhan University**, in 2023.

I was also a **Visiting Student** at the **School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science (EECS), Queen Mary University of London**, from 2021 to 2022. I am currently a **Postdoctoral Researcher** at **Queen Mary University of London** since 2024, and started remotely in September 2023.

My recent research focuses on **Pinching-Antenna Systems (PASS)** and **edge intelligence for next-generation wireless networks**.
